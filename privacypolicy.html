Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

1. Information We Do Not Collect

NexaConvert Pro is a "client-side" utility. This means that all image processing—including filters, rotations, and format conversions—happens entirely within your own web browser. We do not upload, store, or have any access to the images you use.

2. Supported Image Formats

Our tool provides local conversion for the following formats:

  • PNG (Lossless & Transparent)
  • JPG / JPEG (Standard)
  • WEBP (Best for Web)
  • BMP (Raw Bitmap)
  • ICO (Favicon) and GIF (Static)
